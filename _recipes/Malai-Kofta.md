<b>Ingredients</b>
+ 2kg Potatoes
+ 400g Tofu
+ 64g Corn Starch
+ 40g Cilantro congelado
+ 1 Tbsp Lemon Juice
+ 3 Tsp Garam Masala
+ 3 Tsp Salt
+ 2 x Diced Tomatoes (800ml)
+ 34g Cashews
+ 20g Ginger
+ 1 Tsp Turmeric
+ 1 Tsp Cayenne Pepper
+ 400ml Coconut Milk
+ 1 Tbsp Kasoori Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves)
+ 400g Lentils

<b>Instructions</b>
1. Peel and cut the potatoes in half, boil them in water until fully cooked and mash.
1. Add canned tomatoes, cashews, ginger, garam masala (1½ Tsp), turmeric (1 Tsp), cayenne (1 Tsp) and salt (1 Tsp). Let simmer covered for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
1. Blend sauce in a blender, return to the pot and add coconut milk and kasoori methi.
1. Turn the tofu into vegan ricotta.
1. Add tofu ricotta, lentils, mashed potatoes, corn starch, minced cilantro, lemon juice, garam masala (1½ Tsp) and salt (2 Tsp). Form balls and fry.
