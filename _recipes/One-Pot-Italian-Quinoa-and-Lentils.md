<b>Ingredients</b>
+ 400g Lentils
+ 400g Quinoa
+ 50g Sun-dried Tomatoes
+ 1 Tsp Salt
+ 1 Tsp Thyme
+ 1L Vegetable Broth
+ 200g Spinach
+ 1 Tbsp Lemon Juice
+ ½ Tsp Black Pepper
+ 400ml Chopped Tomatoes (Canned)

<b>Instructions</b>
1. Add lentils, quinoa, salt, thyme and broth. Bring to boil, reduce heat, cook for 30 mins.
1. Add tomatoes, spinach, lemon juice, black pepper and salt. Cook and stir for 5 mins.
