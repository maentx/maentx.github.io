<b>Ingredients</b>
+ 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
+ ½ teaspoon cumin
+ ½ teaspoon cinnamon
+ 1 teaspoon salt
+ ½ teaspoon black pepper (ground)
+ 400g Quinoa
+ 500ml Vegetable Broth
+ 400g Chickpeas
+ 100g dried cranberries
+ 33g finely diced flat leaf parsley
+ 64g sliced toasted almonds

<b>Instructions</b>
1. Add oil, turmeric, cumin, cinnamon, salt and pepper to pot and cook (30 secs).
1. Add quinoa and broth, bring to boil, then cover, reduce heat and cook (15 mins).
1. Add chickpeas, cranberries, parsley and mix.
1. Garnish with toasted almonds and parsley.
