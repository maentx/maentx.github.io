<b>Ingredients</b>
+ 1 Small Sweet Potato
+ 1 Green Chili
+ 2 Tsp Ginger (minced)
+ 2 Tsp Garam Masala
+ 2 Tsp Paprika (spicy)
+ Salt & Pepper
+ 400g Quinoa
+ 400g Chickpeas
+ 400g Tomatoes (diced)
+ 400g Tofu
+ 300ml Vegetable Broth

<b>Instructions</b>
1. Heat olive oil, add sweet potato and cook until significantly softened (approx. 10 mins).
1. Add green chili and ginger and cook for another minute. Stir in Garam Masala, Salt, Pepper and Paprika and cook for another 30 seconds.
1. Add quinoa, vegetable broth, chickpeas and tomatoes and stir to combine. Bring to boil and simmer afterwards. Cook until the quinoa and the sweet potato are cooked through, which will take approx. 30 minutes.
1. Serve with some lemon juice and chopped cilantro.
