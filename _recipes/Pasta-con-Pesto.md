<b>Ingredients</b>
+ 500g Pasta
+ 275 Tofu (drained)
+ Cherry Tomatoes
+ 4 Avocados
+ 20g Basil
+ 250g Spinach (frozen)
+ 100g Cashews
+ 1 Tsp Nutritional Yeast
+ 1 Tbsp Salt
+ 1 Garlic Clove
+ Lemon Juice
+ Agua (si hace falta)

<b>Instructions</b>
1. Fry tofu and cook pasta.
1. Add oil, avocados, basil, spinach, cashews and lemon juice into a blender and process.
