<b>Ingredients</b>
+ 1 Tbsp Olive Oil (virgen extra)
+ 1 Onion (diced)
+ 2 Garlic Cloves (minced)
+ 1/2 Red Pepper (chopped)
+ 1/2 Green pepper (chopped)
+ 1 Carrot (diced)
+ 400g Tomatoes (canned)
+ 2 Tbsp Soy Sauce
+ 2 Tsp Oregano (dried)
+ 1 Tsp Thyme (dried)
+ 1 Tsp Garlic Powder
+ 1 Tsp Onion Powder
+ 1 Tsp Ground Cumin
+ 1 Tsp Sweet Paprika
+ 1/8 Tsp Ground Black Pepper
+ 1/8 Tsp Cayenne Powder
+ 400g Quinoa
+ 750 ml Vegetable Broth
+ 400g Chickpeas (cooked)
+ 400g Red Beans (cooked)
+ Parsley (chopped)

<b>Instructions</b>
1. Heat oil in a wok and add onion, garlic, pepper and carrot. Cook for 5 minutes.
1. Add tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes.
1. Add soy sauce, spices, quinoa and vegetable broth. Cook until the quinoa is done.
1. Add chickpeas, lentils and parsley and cook for 2 minutes.
