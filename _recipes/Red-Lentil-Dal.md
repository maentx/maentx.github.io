<b>Ingredients</b>
+ 300g Red Lentils
+ 1 Tbsp Olive Oil (Virgen Extra)
+ 1 Tsp Cumin Seeds
+ 1 Tsp Cinnamon
+ 1 Onion (diced)
+ 1 Green Chili (minced)
+ 1 Garlic Cloves (minced)
+ 1 Tbsp Ginger (minced)
+ 1 Tsp Ground Turmeric
+ 1 Tsp Salt
+ 300g Tomatoes (canned)
+ Cilantro (chopped)
+ Basmati Rice

<b>Instructions</b>
1. Cover the lentils in a pot with 300ml of water until they reach into a creamy consistency. Add more water if needed.
1. Cut the onions into cubes, 
1. Heat oil in a wok, add cumin seeds and heat until they start to crackle., cinnamon and salt and cook for 60 to 90 seconds.
1. Add onion, green chili, garlic and ginger and cook for 4 to 5 minutes.
1. Add turmeric, cardamom, paprika, salt and tomato to the pan and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Add cooked lentils to the mixture, add lemon juice and tofu and stir well.
1. Garnish with cilantro and serve with basmati rice.
