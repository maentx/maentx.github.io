<b>Ingredients</b>
+ 250g Red Lentils
+ 1 Tbsp Olive Oil (Virgen Extra)
+ 1/2 Tsp Cumin Seeds
+ 1/2 Tsp Cinnamon
+ 1 Onion (diced)
+ 1 Green Chili (minced)
+ 2 Garlic Cloves (minced)
+ 1 Tbsp Ginger (minced)
+ 1/2 Tsp Ground Turmeric
+ 1/2 Tsp Ground Cardamom
+ 1/2 Tsp Paprika
+ 1 Tsp Salt
+ 400g Tomatoes (canned)
+ 2 Tsp Lemon Juice
+ Cilantro (chopped)
+ Basmati Rice
+ Tofu

<b>Instructions</b>
1. Cook the lentils in a pot with 1,5l water for 20 minutes. Fry the tofu.
1. Heat oil in a wok, add cumin seeds, cinnamon and salt and cook for 60 to 90 seconds.
1. Add onion, green chili, garlic and ginger and cook for 4 to 5 minutes.
1. Add turmeric, cardamom, paprika, salt and tomato to the pan and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Add cooked lentils to the mixture, add lemon juice and tofu and stir well.
1. Garnish with cilantro and serve with basmati rice.
