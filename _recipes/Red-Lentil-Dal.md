<b>Ingredients</b>
+ 300g Red Lentils
+ 1 Tsp Cumin Seeds
+ 1 Onion (diced)
+ Green Chili (minced)
+ Garlic (minced)
+ Ginger (minced)
+ 1 Tsp Ground Turmeric
+ 1 Tsp Salt
+ 300g Tomatoes
+ Cilantro (chopped)
+ Rice

<b>Instructions</b>
1. Wash the lentils and cover them in a pot with 300ml of water. Add a drop of oil.
1. Add the turmeric and cook until they reach a creamy consistency. Add more water if needed.
1. Heat oil in a wok and cut the onions into cubes.
1. Add cumin seeds and heat until they start to crackle. Add the onions.
1. Prepare the garnish by mincing the green chili, garlic and ginger and add them to the wok.
1. Cut the tomatoes and add them as well.
1. When the lentils reached the desired consistency, mix with garnish, salt and cilantro.
