<b>Ingredients</b>
+ 500g Arroz redondo
+ 8 Mushrooms (champignon)
+ 1 Onion
+ 1L Vegetable Broth
+ 2 Tsp Nutritional Yeast
+ 8 Dried Tomatoes
+ 1 Tsp Salt
+ 1 Tsp Black Pepper
+ 125ml White Wine
+ 250g Broccoli

<b>Instructions</b>
1. Slice mushrooms into slices and fry with oil, salt and black pepper. Put aside.
1. Cut onion into cubes and fry (5 mins).
1. Add rice and fry until transparent.
1. Add white wine and cook until the wine volatilises.
1. Add broccoli and vegetable broth in steps of 125ml. Stir regularly.
1. After 15 to 20 minutes remove rice and add oil, nutritional yeast and mushrooms.
