<b>Ingredients</b>
+ 2 Tsp Garam Masala
+ 4 Tsp Paprika
+ 1 Tsp Coriander
+ ½ Tsp Black Pepper
+ 1 Tsp Salt
+ 1/2 Tsp Turmeric
+ 1 Tsp Cumin
+ 1 Red Bell Pepper
+ 3 Tbsp Nutritional Yeast
+ 6 Tbsp Tomato Paste
+ 400ml Chopped Tomatoes (Canned)
+ 400ml Coconut Milk
+ 180g Mushrooms
+ 400g Chickpeas
+ 1 Tbsp Lemon Juice

<b>Instructions</b>
1. Chop tofu, drain liquid and put aside to dry.
1. Add oil, cumin (1 Tsp) and salt (1 Tsp) into the pan and fry for 1 minute.
1. Add ginger and chili and fry for another minute.
1. Add Garam Masala (2 Tsp), Paprika (4 Tsp), Coriander (1 Tsp), Black Pepper (½ Tsp) and Turmeric (½ Tsp).
1. Add bell pepper, nutritional yeast, tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes.
1. Add tomatoes, coconut milk, mushrooms, chickpeas and lemon juice and cook for 4 minutes.
