<b>Ingredients</b>
+ 1 Tsp Cumin
+ 2 Tsp Salt
+ 1 Tsp Paprika
+ 1 Tsp Turmeric
+ 1 Tsp Garam Masala
+ 20g Cilantro (chopped)
+ 20g Chives (chopped)
+ 25g Ginger (chopped)
+ 1 Green Chili (chopped)
+ 400ml Chopped Tomatoes (Canned)
+ 400ml Sieved Tomatoes (Canned)
+ 400ml Coconut Milk

<b>Instructions</b>
1. Add oil, cumin and salt (1 Tsp) into the pan and fry.
1. Add Ginger, Cilantro, Green Chili, Paprika and Turmeric.
1. Add salt (1 Tsp Salt), chopped and sieved Tomatoes as well as the Coconut Milk. Cook for about 10 minutes until the soup has thickened.
1. Switch off the fire and add Chives and Garam Masala.
