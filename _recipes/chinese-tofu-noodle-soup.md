<b>Ingredients (4 Portions)</b>
+ 400g Tofu
+ Turmeric
+ Soy Sauce
+ 1 Onion
+ 3 Garlic Cloves
+ Ginger
+ 3 Tomatoes
+ Miso Paste
+ 1 liter Water
+ Rice Noodles
+ 2 Tbsp Salt
+ 3 Tbsp Nori Flakes

<b>Instructions</b>
1. Heat oil in a wok. Cut tofu into cubes and fry together with turmeric, salt and soy sauce.
1. Cut the onion into cubes and to the wok.
1. Mince garlic and ginger finely. Add to the wok.
1. Cut the tomato into cubes. Add to the wok together with the miso paste.
1. Add enough boiling water to later cook the rice noodles (around 1 liter).
1. Add the rice noodles and cook until they have softened. Add more water to reach a consistency according to your taste.
1. Add nori flakes and serve.
