<b>Ingredients</b>
+ 2 Large Potatoes (Cubed)
+ 250g Green Beans (Cut into 2.5cm lengths)
+ 1 Green Chili
+ 1 Tsp Turmeric
+ 1 Tsp Cumin
+ Salt & Pepper
+ 400g Lentils
+ 400g Tomatoes (stewed)
+ Seitan
+ Lime juice

<b>Instructions</b>
1. Place potatoes in a pan and add enough water to cover them by 2.5cm. Cook until they are just getting tender (approx. 12 minutes). Add green beans and cook for 5 minutes longer until the beans are bright green and tender. Drain and set aside.
1. Add oil, green chili and spices in the pan and fry until well blended and fragrant.
1. Add potatoes, green beans, tomatoes and lime. Bring to boil and lower heat afterwards. Cook for 7 to 10 minutes.
