<b>Ingredients</b>
+ 250g Flour (50/50 normal and wholemeal)
+ 2 Tsp Ground Flaxseeds
+ 2 Tsp Baking Powder
+ 1/2 Tsp Baking Soda
+ 1/2 Tsp Salt
+ 2 Tbsp Lime Juice
+ 250ml Soy Yogurt & 250ml Water (can be replaced by plant milk)
+ 4 Tsp Sugar
+ Icing Sugar (optional)

<b>Instructions</b>
1. In a bowl mix flour, ground flaxseeds, Baking Powder, Baking Soda, Salt, Lime Juice and Sunflower Oil.
1. Add yogurt and water. Stir until the ingredients are evenly combined.
1. Let the mixture rest for 5 minutes.
1. Add the mixture to a pan and fry until the bottom side is golden brown. Use a spatula to turn and repeat.
1. When done, put the mass on a cutting board and cut into bite-sized pieces.
1. Pour some sunflower oil into the pan and the sugar. Fry until it caramelises, stir regularly.
1. When the sugar is completely caramelised, add the pieces to the pan and fry until they are golden brown from every side.
1. Place pieces on plates and add icing sugar if desired.
1. Serve with a sauce of your own choice (some examples would be apple, strawberry, plum or apricot compote/jam/sauce).
