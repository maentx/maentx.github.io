<b>Ingredients</b>
+ 500ml Water
+ 2 Bananas
+ 100g Rolled Oats
+ 125g Flour (50/50 normal and wholemeal)
+ 1 Tsp Cinnamon
+ 2 Tsp Baking Powder
+ 1/2 Tsp Baking Soda
+ 1/2 Tsp Salt
+ 1 Tbsp Apple Cider or White Vine Vinegar
+ 3 Tbsp Sunflower Oil

<b>Instructions</b>
1. Blend 250ml water and 2 bananas until completely liquid. While blending, add 100g oats and continue blending.
1. In a bowl mix flour, ground flaxseeds, cinnamon, Baking Powder, Baking Soda, Salt, Vinegar and Sunflower Oil.
1. Add the banana-oat-mixture to the bowl. Fill the blender glass with the remaining water, blend and add to bowl.
1. Stir until the ingredients are evenly combined. Let the mixture rest for 5 minutes.
1. Add pancake mixture (2 tablespoons per pancake) to pan and fry until golden brown.
